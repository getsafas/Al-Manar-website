Services

Al Manar's dedicated technical team offers Maintenance Service to the clients on annual contact basis.

1. 1 year free site mainteanance against any manufacturing defects (For Under warranty systems)
2. 1 year Mainteanance upon mutual agreed terms & conditions.
3. Service offered within 24 hours from time call been originated and between 9.00am ~ 5pm on official working days.

Basic services includes the following:
- Free survey and estimation report for any upgrade or expansion or repair
- Fine tuning of existing system.
- Alignment and cleaning surface of lens and front cover.
- Examine the performance of DVR and recorded data.
- Examine performance of devices and life of batteries, if any.

Basic Services excludes:
- New Installation
- Moving or relocating any equipment
- Interchange of new equipment to existing working equipment
- Modification on existing system
- Spare parts (If Al Manar’s warranty period expired)
- Repair of faulty devices (If Al Manar’s warranty period expired)
